Biryani

Description

Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful one-pot rice dish made with meat, aromatic spices, and basmati rice. It's a beloved dish across South Asia.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice - 2 cups
  • Meat (chicken/mutton) - 500g
  • Onions - 2 large
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 3 tbsp
  • Yogurt - 1 cup
  • Ghee - 4 tbsp
  • Cumin seeds - 1 tsp
  • Cinnamon stick - 1
  • Cardamom pods - 4
  • Bay leaves - 2
  • Red chili powder - 1 tsp
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Coriander and mint leaves - handful
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Soak basmati rice in water for 30 minutes.
  2. Fry sliced onions in ghee until golden and crispy. Set aside.
  3. Marinate meat with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices for 30 minutes.
  4. Boil water with whole spices and salt. Add rice and cook until 70% done.
  5. In a heavy-bottomed pot, layer meat mixture, then rice, then fried onions.
  6. Dot with ghee and garnish with coriander and mint leaves.
  7. Cover with foil and then place the lid. Cook on high heat for 2 minutes until steam forms.
  8. Reduce heat to low and cook for 45 minutes.
  9. Turn off heat and let it rest for 5 minutes without opening the lid.
  10. Open carefully and mix gently with a fork. Serve hot.
