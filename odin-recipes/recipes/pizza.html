Pizza

Description

Pizza is a delicious Italian dish consisting of a round, flat base of dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various other toppings. It's baked in an oven until the crust is crispy and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Toppings of your choice (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)

Steps

  1. Mix flour, water, olive oil, salt, and yeast in a bowl to form dough.
  2. Knead the dough for 8-10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  3. Let the dough rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours until it doubles in size.
  4. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  5. Roll out the dough into a round pizza shape and transfer to a pizza pan.
  6. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  7. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  8. Add your desired toppings.
  9. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  10. Remove from oven and let cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
