Lasagna


Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy cheese. It is baked until golden and delicious.

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground Meat
  • Cheese
  • Salt and spices

Instructions

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles.
  2. Prepare the sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese.
  4. Bake in the oven.
